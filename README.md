
# 💼 Purplerain TechSafe – Career Page

## 📦 How to Deploy

1. **Build your project**
   Make sure your final static HTML files are generated (e.g., via Next.js export, or just plain HTML).

2. **Upload to Netlify**

   * Drag-and-drop the output/static folder into [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop)
   * OR push your repo and link it via Netlify dashboard.

3. **Netlify Auto Form Detection**
   Netlify will auto-detect any `<form>` with the attribute `netlify`.

4. **Configure Email Notifications**

   * Go to your site on Netlify
   * Navigate to **Forms** tab
   * Click on the form (e.g., "Apply Now")
   * Enter your **email ID** under the **Form notifications > Send email** section

---

## 📬 Resume Handling

* Uploaded resumes are sent via email (if configured)
* Also visible under the **Forms** tab in Netlify dashboard
* You can export submissions as CSV or forward them to external services
